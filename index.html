My Top 5 Most Inspirational Moments In Sports

Anderson Silva vs Chael Sonnen

Chael was the first person to trash talk Anderson who at the time was considered the #1 pound for pound fighter in the world. The great rivalry and the fact that Chael showed Silva no fear is the reason why this made my top 5. Chael beat Silva the whole night until 1:55 left on the clock when Silva came back out of nowhere submitting Chael and winning the fight.

Gabriela Andersen-Schiess Olympic Marathon

Gabriela was exhausted after a grueling marathon in the 1984 olympics. Despite the fact that she could barely walk her determination pushed her to the finish line. Her actions became an iconic moment in sports.

Derek Redmond

Derek got injured in the beginning of his run. He tore his hamstring. However due to sheer will he did not stop and hopped trying to finish the race. It was then that his father ran onto the track to help his son finish. Then Derek broke down in tears realizing his olympics dreams were over.

Ray Allen 3pt Shot

Ray Allen hit one of the biggest clutch shots in NBA finals history. With less than two minutes left on the clock Ray Allen made the 3 that would eventually tie the game and force a game 7. Ultimately winning the NBA championship.

Lebron James Block Game 7

Lebron James with his signature chase down block was one of the best defensive plays in NBA finals history. Lebron's defense stopped GSW from getting the lead which led to Cavs winning their first championship.
